About Our Team

Welcome to my cocktail blog! My name is Laura, and I am a self-taught mixologist with a passion for creating unique and delicious drinks. I have always been fascinated by the art and science of mixology, and I enjoy experimenting with different ingredients and techniques to come up with new and exciting cocktails.
On my blog, you will find a wide variety of cocktail recipes, from classic drinks like the Old Fashioned and Martini, to more modern and creative concoctions.
Whether you're a seasoned pro or a cocktail novice, I hope you will find my blog to be a valuable resource for all things related to mixology. So come along and join me on this journey of discovery as we explore the delicious world of cocktails together!
